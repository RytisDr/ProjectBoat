We`re all in the Same Boat

What is Project Boat?

Project Boat is a safe digital platform for sharing creative projects within specified groups. It is a place where group members can show their final products, learn new skills from other members and get positive feedback.

For Who?

Project Boat is for everybody, who belongs to a group in the creative field, and wants to share their creations.

How does it work?

Every group member creates a profile on the platform. Everybody can create closed groups of various scopes. Each paticipant has to upload his/her projects before they can see creations of other group members. Groups are encouraged to upload also process documentation to enhance sharing knowledge.

Our Vision

Our vision is to achieve that all the institutions and students in the creative field use our platform for project sharing. We want to be the one of the most recognizable brands to provide safe service, feeling of belonging, share creative thinking and gain a lot of inspiration.
