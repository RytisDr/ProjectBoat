We're in the same boat

We are the company behind the Project Boat, a group project sharing platform for creatives.

It is well known, that curiosity is the mother of all inventions. Curiosity is also one of the key reasons for creating the digital agency “Project Boat”, because people are eager to see what their peers are up to. Project Boat will fulfill this urge by providing a digital sharing platform for students in creative fields.
